01 Introduction

Oz Scholars Academy is committed to protecting your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, store, and protect your personal information.

02 Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Residential suburb
  • Date of birth
  • Parent or guardian details
  • Educational information
  • Account details
  • Billing information

Technical Information

We may collect:

  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Browser information
  • Website activity
  • Cookies
  • Analytics information

03 How We Collect Information

We collect information when you:

  • Create an account
  • Book services
  • Complete forms
  • Subscribe to newsletters
  • Contact us
  • Attend events
  • Use our website

04 How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Deliver tutoring services
  • Match students with tutors
  • Manage bookings
  • Process payments
  • Improve services
  • Respond to enquiries
  • Maintain platform security
  • Send updates and communications

05 Marketing Communications

We may send:

  • Newsletters
  • Educational updates
  • Promotions
  • Event invitations

Users may unsubscribe at any time.

06 Cookies

Cookies may be used to:

  • Improve user experience
  • Remember preferences
  • Measure website traffic
  • Improve website functionality

Users can disable cookies through browser settings.

07 Sharing Information

We may disclose information to:

  • Tutors
  • Contractors
  • Technology providers
  • Payment processors
  • Legal authorities where required

We do not sell personal information.

08 Data Storage & Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect information.

While we take security seriously, no electronic system can guarantee complete security.

09 Overseas Data Transfers

Some providers may store information outside Australia.

By using our services, you consent to these transfers where reasonably necessary.

10 Children's Privacy

We provide services to minors.

Where required, parental consent will be obtained before collecting information from children. For more on how we keep young people safe, see our Child Safety Policy.

11 Access & Correction

Users may request access to their personal information and request corrections to inaccurate information.

12 Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to:

  • Deliver services
  • Meet legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Protect our business interests

13 Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.

Updated versions will be published on this page.
