01 Purpose

This policy outlines how cancellations, rescheduling requests, refunds, and missed sessions are handled by Oz Scholars Academy.

02 Student Cancellations

More than 24 hours notice. Students may cancel or reschedule sessions without penalty when at least 24 hours notice is provided.
Less than 24 hours notice. Where less than 24 hours notice is provided, the session may be forfeited, no refund may be provided, and rescheduling is not guaranteed.

Exceptions may be considered for emergencies.

03 Tutor Cancellations

If a tutor cancels:

  • The session will be rescheduled; or
  • A credit will be provided; or
  • A refund may be issued,

at the discretion of Oz Scholars Academy.

04 Missed Sessions

If a student does not attend a scheduled session without notice:

  • The session will be considered completed
  • No refund will be issued
  • No replacement session is guaranteed

05 Technical Issues

Where technical issues occur:

  • OSA will assess the situation
  • A replacement session may be offered
  • Credits may be issued where appropriate

06 Package Refunds

Unused tutoring credits may be eligible for partial refunds.

Completed sessions are non refundable. Administrative fees may apply.

07 Digital Products

Digital products including:

  • Study guides
  • Downloadable resources
  • Templates
  • Educational materials

are generally non refundable once accessed or downloaded.

08 Events & Workshops

Refunds for events may be available where cancellation occurs at least 7 days before the event.

No refunds may be provided for non attendance.

09 Exceptional Circumstances

OSA may provide refunds outside this policy in exceptional circumstances including:

  • Serious illness
  • Family emergencies
  • Significant service failures

Refund decisions remain at our sole discretion.

10 Contact

Refund requests should be submitted to:

Email: ozscholarsacademy@gmail.com

Please note: This document is provided as a general template. Australian Consumer Law guarantees certain consumer rights that cannot be excluded. We recommend having this policy reviewed by a qualified solicitor before publication.
