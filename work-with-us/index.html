The real perks

What you actually get

No gimmicks — things that genuinely help your teaching, your time, and your career.

Ready-made resources

Lesson templates, diagnostics, a worksheet bank, parent-update and mistake-tracker templates, study planners, and an AI prompt library. You focus on teaching; we give you the structure.

Flexible online work

Teach from anywhere in Australia, with lesson times that fit around uni, work or other commitments. No commute, no fixed roster.

An official certificate

Complete onboarding and earn an official Oz Scholars tutor certificate — proof of the training and standards you've met.

LinkedIn recommendation

After six months of strong performance, we'll write you a genuine LinkedIn recommendation that carries real weight.

Professional reference

High-performing educators get a professional reference for future jobs, study applications or scholarships.

Be part of the story

Get featured in student success stories, and contribute to public resources published under your own name — building your profile as you go.

Who we're looking for

High standards, on purpose

It's a selective team — that's part of why it's worth being on. You'll likely be a strong fit if you have:

Strong academic results in the subjects you'd teach
The knack for explaining hard ideas simply
Reliability, patience and clear communication
A working online teaching setup (laptop, webcam, internet)
A current Working With Children Check — or willingness to get one
A minimum commitment of one term
